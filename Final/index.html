What is DACH?

The DACH countries are the three main European countries that speak German:

  • Germany
  • Austria
  • Switzerland

Why "DACH"?

The name "DACH" is based off of the names for the three countries.

  • D = Germany (in German, Deutschland)
  • A = Austria (in Latin, Austria)
  • CH = Switzerland (in Latin, Confoederatio Helvetica)
