DACH (Germany Austria Switzerland) Countries

A website providing an overview of the three main German-speaking countries: Germany, Austria, and Switzerland (DACH).

This website will showcase different aspects of each the three largest German-speaking countries: Germany, Austria, and Switzerland. Included will be basic information (flag, location, population, etc.), language differences between the countries, food, traditions and holidays, and history. The site’s aim is to introduce its’ visitors to the culture of these three countries. The site will also link to other locations where the visitor can learn more about these cultures.
